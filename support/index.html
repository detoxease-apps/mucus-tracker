Frequently Asked Questions

How do I log my cervical mucus?

Tap the corresponding type button (Dry, Sticky, Creamy, Watery, or Egg-white) on the main screen to log your cervical mucus type for today. You can also drag the stretch slider and write custom notes.

What does the fertile window mean?

The fertile window shows the days when you are most likely to conceive. MucusSync highlights this window based on egg-white or watery cervical mucus, which typically indicates peak fertility.

Is my data private?

Yes. All data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of your data. No account is required.
