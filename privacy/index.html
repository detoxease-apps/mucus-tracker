Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 3, 2026

1. Information We Collect

MucusSync does not collect any personal information. All data you enter — including cervical mucus records, stretch scores, cycle parameters, and notes — is stored exclusively on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or have access to any of your data. No analytics, advertising identifiers, or tracking technologies are used.

2. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device using an on-device database. Your data never leaves your phone. No account creation or sign-in is required to use this app.

3. Third-Party Services

MucusSync does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No data is shared with any third-party service providers.

4. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since all data is stored locally on your device, no data is collected or transmitted by us.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by an updated "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
